Welcome To B. D. Academy

B.D. Academy is established at Amarpur by Sri Rabindra Singh on 1st. Jan. 2007 to commemorate the memory of his elder brother Late Brikodar Singh of village Rajpur. It intend to send out ideal citizens to serve the country and nurture the mankind.
The prime objective of this institution is to provide for its students an environment that would enhance their intellectual competence, religious thinking and make them perfectly fit to sail smoothly through the tumultuous sea of life and contribute their best in the development of nation. The main building of the school is located at Khemichak, about 1.5 kms from Amarpur, on Amarpur-Banka Road. In addition, the primary wing of the school is on the Thana road, Amarpur Banka. The school has a large playground and children are facilitated to play all the indoor and outdoor games. The school is equipped with science and computer laboratories aided with broadband facility, a good library and a well furnished hostel with 24hr power backup and RO mineral water.
