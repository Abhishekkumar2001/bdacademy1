Welcome To B. D. Academy

B.D. Academy is established at Amarpur by Sri Rabindra Singh on 1st. Jan. 2007 to commemorate the memory of his elder brother Late Brikodar Singh of village Rajpur. It intend to send out ideal citizens to serve the country and nurture the mankind. The prime objective of this institution is to provide for its students an environment that would enhance their intellectual competence, religious thinking and make them perfectly fit to sail smoothly through the tumultuous sea of life and contribute their best in the development of nation. The main building of the school is located at Khemichak, about 1.5 kms from Amarpur, on Amarpur-Banka Road. In addition, the primary wing of the school is on the Thana road, Amarpur Banka. The school has a large playground and children are facilitated to play all the indoor and outdoor games. The school is equipped with science and computer laboratories aided with broadband facility, a good library and a well furnished hostel with 24hr power backup and RO mineral water.

Facilities



Library

The library of the school is having more than 5000 books. Books from almost all the leading publications are available like Encyclopedia Britanica, Ramayan Upnishad, Kuran Majeed, Guru Granth Sahib, Puran and Gita, Novels of all leading writers etc.

Science Laboratorioes

There are separate laboratories for Physics, Chemistry, Biology and Mathematics. All the laboratories are well equipped with all the required instruments and chemicals up to +2 level and having facilities of water and electricity.

Computer Lab

The computer lab of the school is having more than 30 computers and having accommodation of 60 students at a time. The broadband connectivity in the computer lab provides exposure to the children to the whole world. Here we provide computer education from std. 1 onwards.
